A Reputation over a decade in the Making

The family has been involved in general automotive repair for over 10 years, and the business continues to grow.

Maxwell Cars is a family owned and operated auto repair service in Nairobi serving our local community and surrounding areas. Our ASE certified technicians are ready to deal with any car repair needed or vehicle issues you may be experiencing. The Maxwell brothers, owners, started the auto repair shop.
